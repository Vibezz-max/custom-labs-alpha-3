FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does customization take?

Most custom orders are completed within 3-5 business days. Rush orders can be accommodated for an additional fee, with some items available in as little as 24 hours.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept most common image formats including JPG, PNG, PDF, AI, and PSD. For best results, we recommend high-resolution files (at least 300 DPI).

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer tiered pricing for bulk orders. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote on orders of 25+ items.

What is your return policy?

We stand behind our quality. If there's a production error or defect, we'll replace your item at no charge. For custom orders, we can't accept returns due to change of mind, but we work with you during design to ensure you're 100% satisfied.
